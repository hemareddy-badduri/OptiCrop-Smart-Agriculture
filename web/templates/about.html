About OptiCrop

OptiCrop is a machine-learning-powered smart agricultural recommendation system that helps farmers, researchers, and agribusinesses choose the most suitable crop for a given set of soil and climate conditions.

The problem

Poor crop selection, inefficient resource use, and changing environmental conditions reduce farming productivity. Farmers often cannot easily determine which crop fits their soil nutrients and local climate, leading to lower yields and financial losses.

Our approach

OptiCrop analyses seven key parameters — Nitrogen (N), Phosphorous (P), Potassium (K), temperature, humidity, soil pH, and rainfall — and uses supervised machine learning to predict the best-matching crop. We compare several algorithms (Logistic Regression, K-Nearest Neighbors, Decision Tree, Random Forest) and deploy the best-performing one. K-Means clustering is also used to discover natural groupings of environmental conditions.

Features analysed

  • N — Nitrogen content (kg/ha)
  • P — Phosphorous content (kg/ha)
  • K — Potassium content (kg/ha)
  • temperature — in °C
  • humidity — relative %
  • ph — soil pH value
  • rainfall — in mm

Impact

By recommending the right crop for the right conditions, OptiCrop supports higher yields, optimised use of water and fertilisers, reduced crop-failure risk, and more sustainable, precision-agriculture practices.
